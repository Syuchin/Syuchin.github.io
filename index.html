Biography

Hello! I'm CHYang.

I am a final-year undergraduate student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), under the guidance of Prof. Nenghai Yu and Prof. Qi Chu.

Research Interests: My primary focus lies in Artificial Intelligence Security, Multimodal Agents, and Hallucination Mitigation in Multimodal LLMs. I am particularly passionate about exploring the intersection of AI safety and multimodal systems.
